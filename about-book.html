Mantras of Happy Life explores how inner states shape our everyday outcomes more than skills, strategy, or intelligence

The book introduces the 4S framework as a way to understand leadership behaviours—your own (Self) and others'—without judgement, labels, or blame.

The Problem It Solves

Everyone today face:

  • Constant pressure to perform
  • Conflicting expectations from teams, family, and society
  • Emotional exhaustion masked as productivity
  • Success without satisfaction

Most frameworks address what to do.
This book focuses on where you are operating from and that
why I call it A Story of Everyone.

The 4 States Explained

Survival: Fear-driven mindset focused on safety and control

Superiority: Ego-driven mindset focused on validation and dominance

Self-Attainment: Growth-driven mindset focused on responsibility and clarity

Spirituality: Alignment-driven mindset focused on perspective and balance

Each state has lengths and each has blind spots too.

Author Note

This book emerged from years of observing how capable people struggle —not due to lack of talent, or resources but lack of inner clarity.

The intent is simple:
to offer a language and lens that helps leaders understand themselves before trying to fix others.

The bigger goal is to share a tool that helps you in Becoming your own coach

If these ideas resonate, the newsletter is where I share ongoing reflections while the book journey takes shape.
